10/21/22

LiteTools is being discontinued soon.

It's time to move on from LiteTools. It was an amazing experience working on LiteTools, but I think the time has come. Honestly, I wasn't expecting for LiteTools to get across the world, literally! I personally feel like starting a business over software that an amatuer programmer created won't go that far in the long run. It was a great experience, and I will definitely be dipping my feet in these waters again once I get more experienced and I know what I'm doing. A business cannot run on free open source software since no revenue can be made from it, and the fact there are mostly better alternatives to the things I develop anyways. Also you can't run a business all by yorself. I will be moving all of my LiteTools repositories to my personal profile since I will maintain Tasks and the other projects.

I might go back to LiteTools one day. Again, thank you everyone for helping me through this journey.
