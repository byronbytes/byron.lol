Also you can't run a business all by yorself. I will be moving all of my LiteTools repositories to my personal profile since I do want to maintain Tasks and the other projects.

Not much will change from this, and I'll still be keeping my custom email because ~its cool~ I might go back to LiteTools one day. Again, thank you everyone for helping me through this journey.
